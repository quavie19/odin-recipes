Jerk Chicken

Fried Jerk Chicken Wings

These jerk chicken wings were so flavorful, so different, and so additively delicious, that I may have eaten my last Buffalo wing. Okay, I didn't mean that, but these really are amazing; a perfect balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy.

I'm usually pretty casual about you changing my recipes, but I'm going to have to insist you not change any of the key ingredients, which are, every one of them.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Yellow Onion Chopped
  • 1/2 Cup Green Onions Sliced
  • 6 Cloves Garlic
  • 3 Habanero Peppers
  • 1/2 Cup Lime Jucie
  • 3 Pounds Chicken Wing Drummets
  • Cooking Spray
